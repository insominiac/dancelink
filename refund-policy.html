Refund & Payment Policy

At DanceLink, we strive to provide exceptional dance education and experiences. We understand that sometimes plans change, and we've designed our refund policy to be fair and transparent while ensuring the sustainability of our programs.

Detailed Refund Policies

🎯 Classes and Packages

Single Classes

  • Full refund if cancelled at least 24 hours before class start time
  • 50% refund if cancelled 12-24 hours before class
  • No refund for cancellations less than 12 hours before class
  • Option to transfer to another class within 30 days

Class Packages (5, 10, or 20 classes)

  • Full refund if no classes have been attended and cancellation is made within 14 days of purchase
  • Pro-rated refund minus 20% administrative fee after first class
  • No refunds after attending 3 or more classes
  • Packages are valid for 6 months from purchase date
  • One-time package freeze available for up to 30 days

Package Transfer Policy

Class packages can be transferred to another person once during the validity period with a $25 transfer fee.

🎓 Workshops and Intensives

14+ days before

100% refund or credit towards future workshop

7-13 days before

75% refund or 100% credit towards future workshop

3-6 days before

50% refund or 75% credit towards future workshop

Less than 3 days

No refund, but 50% credit if space can be filled

Note: For multi-day intensives, partial attendance does not qualify for partial refunds.

🏆 Events and Competitions

Social Dance Events

  • Full refund if cancelled 72+ hours before event
  • 50% refund if cancelled 24-72 hours before
  • No refund within 24 hours of event
  • Tickets are transferable to another person

Competitions and Congresses

  • Registration fees are non-refundable after 14 days before event
  • Partner substitutions allowed with $25 administrative fee
  • Category changes permitted until 7 days before event
  • Medical emergencies considered on case-by-case basis with documentation

👤 Private Lessons

Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation or rescheduling 48+ hours before lesson
  • One-time emergency cancellation allowed with less than 48 hours notice per month
  • No-shows forfeit the lesson fee
  • Instructor cancellations result in full refund or free makeup lesson

Lesson Packages

  • 5-lesson packages: Valid for 3 months
  • 10-lesson packages: Valid for 6 months
  • Unused lessons expire and are non-refundable
  • One package extension of 30 days available for $50

🔄 Membership Subscriptions

Monthly Subscriptions

  • Cancel anytime before next billing cycle
  • No partial month refunds
  • Pause subscription for up to 3 months per year
  • Downgrade or upgrade anytime, effective next billing cycle

Annual Subscriptions

  • 30-day money-back guarantee from purchase date
  • After 30 days, no refunds available
  • One-time pause for up to 2 months
  • Transfer to monthly plan with price adjustment

Payment Methods & Security

💳 Accepted Payment Methods

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit Cards with Visa/MasterCard logo
  • PayPal and PayPal Credit
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Venmo (for in-person transactions)
  • Bank transfers (for packages over $500)
  • Payment plans available for programs over $300

Payment Security

All payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment processors. We never store your full credit card information on our servers.

Special Circumstances

🏥 Medical Emergencies

We understand that medical emergencies happen. With proper documentation:

  • Full refund for single classes and events
  • Pro-rated refund for packages and subscriptions
  • Extended validity period for prepaid services
  • Documentation must be provided within 7 days

🌍 Force Majeure

In cases of natural disasters, pandemics, or government-mandated closures:

  • Services will be suspended, not cancelled
  • Online alternatives offered where possible
  • Automatic extension of all packages and subscriptions
  • Option for credit or refund if closure exceeds 30 days

✈️ Travel and Relocation

If you're relocating or traveling:

  • Packages can be frozen for up to 3 months with proof of travel
  • Subscriptions can be paused or cancelled
  • Transfer to online classes available
  • Partner studio credits in select cities

How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Submit Request

Email refunds@dancelink.com with your order number and reason for refund

Step 2: Review Process

Our team will review your request within 2 business days

Step 3: Confirmation

You'll receive an email confirming approval or requesting additional information

Step 4: Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

Step 5: Receipt

Funds appear in your account within 3-5 business days after processing

Required Information for Refund Requests

  • Full name and email used for purchase
  • Order/Transaction number
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund request
  • Supporting documentation (if applicable)

Additional Terms

Currency and Taxes

  • All prices are in USD unless otherwise specified
  • Applicable taxes will be added at checkout
  • International customers responsible for currency conversion fees
  • Tax-exempt organizations must provide documentation

Disputes and Chargebacks

  • Please contact us before initiating a chargeback
  • Unwarranted chargebacks may result in account suspension
  • We reserve the right to collect on disputed amounts
  • Legal fees may be added to unresolved disputes

Policy Updates

This refund policy may be updated periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email. The policy in effect at the time of purchase applies to that transaction.
