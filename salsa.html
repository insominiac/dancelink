The Evolution of Salsa

From Cuban roots to global phenomenon

1920s-1940s

Cuban Origins

Born from the fusion of Son Cubano, Mambo, and Afro-Cuban rhythms in the streets of Havana

1960s-1970s

New York Evolution

Puerto Rican and Cuban immigrants in New York City transform the dance, creating the distinctive NY style

1980s-1990s

Global Expansion

Salsa spreads worldwide, with distinct regional styles emerging in Colombia, LA, and Europe

2000s-Present

Modern Fusion

Contemporary Salsa incorporates elements from other dances while maintaining its authentic Latin soul
